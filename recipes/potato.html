Boiled Egg

Description

Theres not much that can beat a well roasted spud

Ingredients

  • potato
  • water
  • thyme
  • oil

Steps

  1. wash and boil the potato for 10mins
  2. drain the potato
  3. add some oil in the pot and fry a bit
  4. transfer to a baking tray and put in oven at high temp
  5. take out of the oven when golden and crisp, and then allow to cool
